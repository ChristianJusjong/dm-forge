Introduction

Welcome to DM Codex ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using DM Codex:

  • Party member information (character names, classes, levels)
  • NPC data and generated content
  • Session notes and campaign details
  • Initiative tracker data
  • Encounter configurations

Important: All this data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We do not collect, store, or transmit this data to our servers.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Browser type, device information, pages visited, time spent on pages
  • Cookies: We use cookies for language preferences and ad serving (see section on Advertising)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the DM Codex service
  • Improving user experience and functionality
  • Displaying relevant advertisements (via Google AdSense)
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance our features

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

3.2 AI Services

We use the following AI services for content generation:

  • Groq API: For NPC generation, translations, and content suggestions
  • Google Gemini: Fallback AI service for content generation

When you use AI-powered features, your prompts and inputs are sent to these services. We recommend not including personally identifiable information in your AI requests.

3.3 Open5e API

We use Open5e API to fetch D&D 5e SRD monster data. No personal information is transmitted to this service.

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Your campaign data, NPCs, encounters, and session notes are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Data never leaves your device
  • We cannot access your campaign information
  • Clearing browser data will delete your saved information
  • Data is not synced across devices

5. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights:

  • Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of data processing
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Right to Object: Object to our processing of your data

6. Cookies Policy

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Language preferences, localStorage data
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense advertising

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

DM Codex is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
